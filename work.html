Research {14-05-2024}

Polymer Science

This study looked at how different chemicals (sulfur and peroxide) affect the properties of a blend of two materials: epoxidized natural rubber (ENR) and ethylene vinyl acetate (EVA). The results showed that using sulfur improved the strength and durability of the blend compared to using peroxide. The sulfur-treated blend cured faster and had better resistance to wear and tear. These findings suggest that this blend could be used to make durable rubber products like O-rings and gaskets with further development. Overall, the sulfur method produced materials with superior performance. For more click here.
